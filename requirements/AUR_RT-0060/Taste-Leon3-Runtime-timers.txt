TASTE Leon3 Runtime uses Timer module for accessing Leon3 hardware timers, as per chapter 5.1 of [1].
Timer module is part of Leon3-BSP, as per chapter 5.2 of [1]. Leon3 hardware timers provide resolution 
of 50 ns, as per [2].
